Our Story

Why Sif's Gold Exists

The beauty industry is full of talented, hardworking people — students grinding through school, licensed professionals building their clientele, school owners managing everything at once, and clients who just want to find someone they trust.

But the tools available to them? Scattered, outdated, and built for every industry except this one.

Sif's Gold was created to change that. Named after the Norse goddess of golden hair — a symbol of beauty, abundance, and resilience — this platform is a home for every person who makes beauty their life's work.

We're building for the students who haven't graduated yet. For the booth renter doing everything alone. For the school director who deserves better software. For the client who's tired of starting over.
